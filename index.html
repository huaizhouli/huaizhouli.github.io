    "Hello — morning, noon, and night!"¶

    Hi there, and welcome! I’m a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Finance at the Tuck School of Business, Dartmouth College, with a PhD from UNSW Business School. My research interests include corporate finance, product market competition, entrepreneurial finance, and international trade. My work has been presented at conferences such as CICF, FMA, FIRN, and AFBC.
