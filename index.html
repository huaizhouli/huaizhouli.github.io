    Huaizhou Li

    Huaizhou Li, is a PhD Candidate in Finance at the School of Banking and Finance, UNSW Business School. His research interests include corporate finance, product market competition, entrepreneurial finance and international trade. His work has been accepted for presentation at conferences such as CICF, FMA, FIRN, and AFBC.

    Education

    • 2020 - current Ph.D. in Finance, University of New South Wales
    • 2019 - 2020 Pre-Doc in Finance, University of New South Wales
    • 2016 - 2018 MPhil in Finance, University of New South Wales
    • 2012 - 2016 Bachelor of Economics (major in Finance), Nanjing Normal University
